What is the SAT?

The SAT is a standardized test widely used for college admissions in the United States. It measures literacy, numeracy, and writing skills needed for academic success in college.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Who should take the SAT?

Students planning to apply to colleges or universities that require the SAT as part of their admissions process.

How often can I take the SAT?

You can take the SAT as many times as you want. Most students take it 2-3 times.

What is a good SAT score?

A good SAT score depends on the requirements of your target colleges. Generally, a score above 1200 is considered competitive.
