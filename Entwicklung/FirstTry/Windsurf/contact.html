Frequently Asked Questions

What is the SAT?

The SAT is a standardized test widely used for college admissions in the United States. It tests reading, writing, and math skills.

How long is the SAT?

The SAT is 3 hours long, plus 50 minutes for the optional essay section.

What score do I need?

Required scores vary by university. Top universities typically look for scores above 1400 out of 1600.

How many times can I take the SAT?

There's no limit to how many times you can take the SAT, but it's recommended to take it no more than 3 times.
