Universities Requiring SAT

Explore top universities that require SAT scores for admission

Harvard University

Ivy League Private
Cambridge, Massachusetts
Min SAT: 1520
Acceptance Rate: 3.4%

One of the world's most prestigious universities, Harvard offers exceptional programs across all disciplines with renowned faculty and extensive research opportunities.

Admission Requirements:

  • SAT: 1520+ (required)
  • Subject Tests: Recommended
  • Essays: Required
  • Letters of Recommendation: 2-3

Stanford University

Private Research
Stanford, California
Min SAT: 1510
Acceptance Rate: 3.9%

Leading research university known for innovation and entrepreneurship, particularly strong in technology, engineering, and business programs.

Admission Requirements:

  • SAT: 1510+ (required)
  • Subject Tests: Optional
  • Essays: Required
  • Letters of Recommendation: 2

Massachusetts Institute of Technology

Private STEM
Cambridge, Massachusetts
Min SAT: 1540
Acceptance Rate: 4.1%

World-renowned for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics programs with cutting-edge research facilities and innovation labs.

Admission Requirements:

  • SAT: 1540+ (required)
  • Subject Tests: Math + Science (required)
  • Essays: Required
  • Letters of Recommendation: 2

University of California, Berkeley

Public Research
Berkeley, California
Min SAT: 1420
Acceptance Rate: 14.5%

Top public research university with excellent programs in engineering, business, and liberal arts, known for academic excellence and social activism.

Admission Requirements:

  • SAT: 1420+ (required)
  • Subject Tests: Optional
  • Essays: Required (4 essays)
  • Letters of Recommendation: None required

University of Michigan

Public Big Ten
Ann Arbor, Michigan
Min SAT: 1370
Acceptance Rate: 20.2%

Prestigious public research university with strong programs across all disciplines, excellent alumni network, and vibrant campus life.

Admission Requirements:

  • SAT: 1370+ (required)
  • Subject Tests: Optional
  • Essays: Required
  • Letters of Recommendation: 1

Duke University

Private Research
Durham, North Carolina
Min SAT: 1510
Acceptance Rate: 6.2%

Elite private research university known for academic excellence, strong athletics program, and beautiful Gothic Revival campus.

Admission Requirements:

  • SAT: 1510+ (required)
  • Subject Tests: Optional
  • Essays: Required
  • Letters of Recommendation: 2-3

Northwestern University

Private Big Ten
Evanston, Illinois
Min SAT: 1490
Acceptance Rate: 7.0%

Highly ranked private research university with strong programs in journalism, engineering, business, and performing arts.

Admission Requirements:

  • SAT: 1490+ (required)
  • Subject Tests: Optional
  • Essays: Required
  • Letters of Recommendation: 2

Rice University

Private STEM
Houston, Texas
Min SAT: 1520
Acceptance Rate: 8.7%

Small, highly selective research university known for excellent STEM programs, tight-knit community, and unique residential college system.

Admission Requirements:

  • SAT: 1520+ (required)
  • Subject Tests: Recommended for STEM
  • Essays: Required
  • Letters of Recommendation: 2
