Tips for Accurate Results

Follow these guidelines to get the most accurate score prediction from our calculator.

1

Use Official Practice Tests

Enter scores from official College Board practice tests for the most accurate predictions.

2

Take Tests Under Timed Conditions

Simulate real test conditions including time limits and breaks for realistic scoring.

3

Count Only Correct Answers

The SAT doesn't penalize incorrect answers, so only count questions you answered correctly.

4

Review Your Mistakes

Use the improvement suggestions to focus your study efforts on areas that need the most work.

5

Track Your Progress

Save your results and retake the calculator regularly to monitor your improvement over time.

6

Consider Multiple Tests

Take several practice tests and average the results for a more reliable score prediction.
